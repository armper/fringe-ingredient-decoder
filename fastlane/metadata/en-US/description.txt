Fringe Ingredient Decoder helps you make sense of ingredient lists in seconds.

Open the app, tap Scan barcode when you want camera lookup, or paste ingredients directly from the same home screen. Results are designed to be understood at a glance, with a fast score, a short summary, and a clean ingredient list you can tap through.

What you get:
- barcode lookup powered by Open Food Facts
- support for food and beauty products
- quick summaries like Mostly Simple, Mixed Bag, or Many Additives
- ingredient-by-ingredient tagging for additives, preservatives, sweeteners, colorings, emulsifiers, stabilizers, surfactants, fragrance, and more
- short plain-English ingredient explanations
- broader offline ingredient matching from a bundled reference library built from Open Food Facts, Open Beauty Facts, and FDA inactive-ingredient data
- silent improvement for some unknown ingredients using public taxonomy suggestions, with better matches cached locally on your device
- recent scans and manual entries saved locally for quick reopen

Fringe Ingredient Decoder is built to feel calm, fast, and obvious. No account. No onboarding wall. No dense dashboards.
